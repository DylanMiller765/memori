# First TikTok Video — "Day 0: Can I Get 10,000 Users in 30 Days?"

## Video Concept: The 30-Day Challenge (Day 0)

**Format:** Journey / challenge series opener
**Length:** 30–45 seconds (short = higher completion rate = algorithm boost)
**Goal:** Set up a series people HAVE to follow. Day 0 creates the stakes. Every future video has a built-in audience waiting for the next update.
**Vibe:** Raw, real, slightly vulnerable — a solo dev betting on himself

---

## The Script

### HOOK (0–2 seconds)
**[You filming yourself at your desk/setup. Text on screen: "Day 0"]**

> "I'm a solo developer and I just launched my first app. Can I get 10,000 users in 30 days?"

*(Say this fast and direct. No intro, no "hey guys", no warmup. Just straight into it.)*

### THE APP (2–12 seconds)
**[Quick screen recording clips of Memori — show the flashiest parts]**

Show in rapid succession (1.5–2 sec each):
1. The Brain Score assessment in action (numbers flashing, tapping)
2. The score reveal screen (Brain Score, Brain Age, percentile)
3. The home screen with streak, level, daily workout
4. One game that looks visually satisfying (Visual Memory grid or Color Match)

> *Voiceover:*
> "It's a brain training app called Memori. It gives you a Brain Score out of 1000 and trains your memory, speed, and focus with science-backed games."

### THE STAKES (12–25 seconds)
**[Back on camera. Be real.]**

> "Right now I have zero users. Zero downloads. I'm gonna document everything — what works, what flops, the real numbers. No BS."

**[Flash your App Store Connect showing 0 downloads, or your analytics dashboard at zero. This is the money shot — proof you're starting from nothing.]**

> "If I hit 10K in 30 days I'll [pick a stake — see options below]. If I don't... well, you'll all get to watch me fail publicly."

### THE CLOSE (25–35 seconds)
**[On camera, genuine energy]**

> "Day 0. Follow if you wanna see how this goes."

**[Text on screen: "Day 0 / 10,000" with a progress bar at 0%]**

*(No hard sell. No "link in bio." Not yet. Day 0 is about the STORY, not the download. The app sells itself once people are invested in your journey.)*

## Stakes Options (Pick One)

Pick something that raises the drama. Ideas:

| Stake | Why it works |
|-------|-------------|
| "I'll quit my job" | Highest stakes, most dramatic (only if true-ish) |
| "I'll get a brain score tattoo" | Absurd + memorable |
| "I'll give the app away free for a year" | Generous, makes people root for you |
| "I'll do whatever the comments say" | Interactive, drives engagement |
| "I'll open-source the entire codebase" | Dev community will love this |

Or flip it — don't set a consequence. Just say "Let's see what happens." Mystery works too.

## Production Notes

### What You Need
- iPhone for screen recording (Settings > Control Center > Screen Recording)
- Camera (iPhone front camera is fine) for face-cam clips
- Your desk/setup in the background (makes it feel real, not produced)
- App Store Connect open to show the zero state

### Filming Tips
- **Don't overthink the quality.** Slightly messy desk, natural lighting, phone propped up = perfect. TikTok punishes content that looks like an ad.
- **Talk TO the camera like it's one person**, not an audience. Imagine you're FaceTiming a friend and telling them about this crazy thing you're doing.
- **Film 3 takes of the hook.** The first 2 seconds decide everything. Try different energy levels and pick the one that feels most natural but still grabs attention.
- **Show your face.** TikTok's algorithm boosts faces. Don't hide behind screen recordings the whole time.

### Editing (CapCut)
- Fast cuts — nothing longer than 3 seconds
- Text overlays: "Day 0", "0 users", "10,000 goal", "30 days"
- Add a **progress bar graphic** (0/10,000) — you'll update this every video. This becomes your visual signature for the series.
- Trending sound at LOW volume (15-20%) under your voice
- No transitions, no effects, no filters. Raw.

### Caption
```
Day 0. Solo dev. Zero users. Can I hit 10K in 30 days? Follow for the journey. #indiedev #solodev #appdev #day0 #10kusers #braintraining #startup #buildinpublic
```

### Post Settings
- **When:** Evening (6-9 PM) tends to perform best for story content
- **Reply to EVERY comment** in the first 2 hours. This is non-negotiable.
- **Pin a comment** from yourself: "Predictions? Think I'll make it?"

## Why "Day 0" is the Perfect First Video

1. **Series format = built-in retention.** People follow to see what happens next. Every future video has a warm audience.
2. **Underdog story.** TikTok LOVES a solo dev vs the world narrative. People root for you.
3. **Zero-to-something is universally compelling.** Same reason people watch Shark Tank, startup docs, and build-in-public threads.
4. **No selling required.** You're not pushing an app — you're starting a journey. The app sells itself once people care about YOUR story.
5. **Real numbers = trust.** Showing 0 downloads makes everything that follows believable.
6. **Comment bait is baked in.** "Think I'll make it?" — everyone has an opinion. Comments = algorithm fuel.
7. **Every future video writes itself.** Day 1, Day 3, Day 7, Day 14, Day 30. You always have content.

This first video sets up the entire series. Here's how the journey unfolds:

| Day | Video | Focus |

|-----|-------|-------|

### Week 4: The Final Push
| Day | Video | Focus |
|-----|-------|-------|
| 23 | "7 days left. Here's where I'm at." | Urgency, rally the audience |

**Post daily or every other day minimum.** Not every video needs to be a "day update" — mix in standalone brain training content between updates.

## The Real Strategy

The video itself is just the start. Here's what actually gets you to 10K:

1. **TikTok is the top of funnel.** It gets eyeballs. The App Store listing converts them.
2. **Post on multiple platforms.** Cross-post to Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and Twitter/X. Same video, different audiences.
3. **Reddit r/indiedev, r/solodev, r/iOSProgramming** — post your journey there too. Dev communities love build-in-public.
4. **The series format compounds.** Video 1 might get 500 views. But by Day 15, people who saw Video 1 are following and engaging with every post, which tells the algorithm to push you harder.
5. **Brain Score is your growth hack.** Every person who takes the assessment and shares their score is free marketing. Make sharing dead simple in the app.
